Oliver, the angry orange cat, drove aimlessly through the rain, his loneliness gnawing at him. The empty roads blurred by, but no amount of speed could outrun his sadness.

He stopped on a bridge, staring into the dark, swirling river below. In that quiet moment, the anger that had fueled him faded, leaving only the weight of his solitude.

He turned off the engine, and as the rain poured down, he finally let his tears fall.
